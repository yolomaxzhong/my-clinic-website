Field of Study

How Chinese medicine approaches pain and healing.

Traditional Chinese Medicine views pain as an imbalance in circulation, function, and internal harmony. Treatment is individualized to restore smooth flow and support the body's natural healing response.

Acupuncture

Fine needles stimulate specific points to reduce pain signaling, ease tension, and support recovery.

Tui Na (Therapeutic Bodywork)

Manual techniques improve circulation and release myofascial tightness linked to chronic discomfort.

Cupping Therapy

Gentle suction lifts tissue to improve blood flow, decrease stiffness, and reduce soreness.

Moxibustion

Therapeutic warmth helps with cold-pattern pain, low circulation, and recovery support.

Herbal Medicine

Formula-based support tailored to your constitution, symptoms, and treatment response.

Lifestyle Integration

Simple breathing, movement, sleep, and nutrition habits reinforce treatment between visits.
